Artiol IZRI

Share

https://durresbiennale.org/en/artists/Albania/artists/Artiol-Izri

Artiol IZRI was born in Durrës. He is an artist and illustrator, who has shown a special passion for painting since childhood. In 2019, he graduated in painting at the "Naim Frashëri" Artistic High School in Vlora, specializing in scenography. The years of studying painting at the Artistic High School of Vlora, would take the first steps for his professional engagement in the field of visual art.
In 2019, he moved to study at the Florence Academy of Fine Arts in Italy, where he graduated in painting in 2022 and, currently, is pursuing a postgraduate master's degree in illustration and graphic design. Throughout his artistic career, he has exhibited in many national and international exhibitions in Albania, Italy and elsewhere. These experiences have helped him, not only in his artistic growth, but also in the recognition of his creativity at professional levels.
Artioli develops his artistic activity between Florence and Albania, transforming this interweaving of academic training, practical experience and dedication to visual art and illustration into a stimulating relationship full of opportunities for the development of his artistic career. This approach makes him determined to continue his growth and formation as a professional in the field of art, illustration and graphic design, contributing his creativity and experience to the "landscape" of contemporary Albanian art.


Resonance
Digital painting, 100 x 100 cm, 2024


  • The conceptual approach:


    Based on the practice of digital painting, this work is created entirely thanks to the use of advanced technology. Such a way has made the artistic work to investigate the interrelationships of contemporary themes that encourage today's artist, to reflect and then to concretize them, in an artistic image.
    The work focuses on various aspects of artificial intelligence (AI) and its dynamic interaction with human emotions, future possibilities and perceptual changes. By examining the emotional dimensions of AI, I aim to bridge the gap between human experiences and technological advances, thereby provoking thought about the future of these interactions. All this is possible thanks to the digital artistic devices that allow me to confess through them, experimenting with the variety of colors, the use of brushes, materials and other virtual elements, to reach the consciousness and ability of the human being.
    In this perspective, this work, as well as my work as a whole, investigates the complex interrelationships that exist between physical nature and technological virtuality. This investigation is reflected in the compositions which juxtapose organic elements modeled with technological constructs, simultaneously revealing the harmony and tension inherent in the coexistence of these two dimensions. Through digital painting, I aim to stimulate a discussion that challenges traditional perceptions and invites the public to think about the evolving dynamics of our world. Such a synthesized approach to the interpretation of physical truth through AI presents a nuanced perspective on the symbiotic, and often paradoxical, relationship between real nature and technological innovation.
