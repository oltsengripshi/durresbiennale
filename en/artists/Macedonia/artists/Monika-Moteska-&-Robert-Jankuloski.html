Monika Moteska & Robert Jankuloski

Share

Monika Moteska


Monika MOTESKA was born in 1971 in Prilep, North Macedonia. In 1996, she graduated in painting at the Faculty of Fine Arts in Skopje, and in the same faculty, she defended her master's degree in 2022. Her creativity is interwoven between painting, photography, video and installation. She is the author of twenty-one solo exhibitions where we highlight: North Macedonian Pavilion - 59th Edition Venice Art Biennale (2022); Principij Gallery, Rijeka, Croatia (2022); NGM Cifte Hammam, Skopje (2020/21); Contemporary Art Center, Mobile Gallery, Skopje (2019); Museum of Contemporary Art Vojvodina, Novi Sad, Serbia (2018); MC Gallery, New York, USA (2013) and simultaneously participating in many joint exhibitions.


Robert Jankuloski


Robert JANKULOSKI was born in 1969 in Prilep, North Macedonia. He graduated in camera at the Faculty of Dramatic Arts in Skopje and in 2011, defended his Master of Science in Arts at the University of Audiovisual Arts - ESRA Paris-Skopje-New York, Skopje. In 2000, he founded the Macedonian Center for Photography. He is the author of forty solo exhibitions where we highlight: North Macedonian Pavilion - 59th Edition, Art Biennale in Venice (2022); Principij Gallery, Rijeka, Croatia (2022); National Gallery of North Macedonia (2020/21); Contemporary Art Center, Mobile Gallery, Skopje (2018); Museum of Contemporary Art, Novi Sad, Serbia (2018); Contemporary Art Center, Podgorica, Montenegro (2017) and simultaneously participating in many joint exhibitions in: Belgrade, Vienna, New York, Istanbul, Brussels, Warsaw, Zagreb, Pancevo, Paris, Rome, Plovdiv, Lyon and Rotterdam. Jankuloski has been awarded several prizes in the field of visual arts and film. He has been decorated by the French Ministry of Culture with the title "Knight of Arts and Letters". He is currently a professor at the Faculty of Fine Arts, near Ss. Cyril and Methodius University in Skopje, North Macedonia.


Landscape Experience
Video installation, format 16:9, 8 min, 2022


  • The conceptual approach:


    The experience of is an international trans-tactical project which, through a critical-discursive way, points out the dangers present if serious systematic steps are not taken to overcome the greatest challenges of the Anthropocene, which present a tax on capitalism and ruling politics, such as the uncontrolled abuse of planet Earth's natural resources in the form of senseless wars (direct or indirect, explicit or implicit, real or psychological, physical or chemical) in the name of ideals of one, in which we are all victims, especially the little ones, the innocent and the idealistic. Can the beautiful be extremely ugly at the same time, the aesthetic aspect become the servant of evil, but with the aim of finding the right way to draw attention to it? All these issues, reviewing the thesis of the banality of evil, we find in the artistic work Experience of landscape.
