Victoria Thomen

Share

https://durresbiennale.org/en/artists/Domenican Republic/artists/Victoria-Thomen

Victoria THOMEN was born in 1974 in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic. She is a Dominican-Italian painter, photographer, architect and cultural manager. In 2003 he graduated in architecture at the Pedro Henríquez Ureña National University in Santo Domingo and in 2004, he completed his postgraduate studies in cultural project management. In 2018 he defended his master's degree in artistic photography at the Lens School of Visual Arts in Madrid, Spain. Thomen's art has been exhibited in various places around the world, where we mention her participation in the Architecture Biennale (2012) and PhotoEspaña (2017). She currently works between Rome, Madrid and Santo Domingo, combining her creativity between contemporary photography, conceptual art, installation, video and collage.


Puncture wound / Forare Ferida
Video-art, 1:50 min, 2023


  • The conceptual approach:


    This video art seeks to express, through the metaphor of a wound, the impact that rapid urban development has on the environment. The project demonstrates the relentless drilling of the earth by construction machinery, symbolizing the scars left by the city's rampant expansion. The first part of the video art highlights the frantic pace of urban construction and its harmful effects on nature, including the demolition of abandoned sites. The second part reveals the resilience of nature, showing how flora and fauna slowly reclaim these urban wounds, transforming them into new ecosystems. The work aims to raise awareness of the environmental consequences of uncontrolled urbanization and the potential that nature has to restore the denied balance.
